## AI & photo disclosure

Altered media and undisclosed AI create real liability as of 2026.

- **AI-altered / virtually-staged photos must be disclosed.** Label them "Virtually Staged" on the image and
  in the listing (NAR Code of Ethics Art. 2 & 12; most MLS rules; CA AB 723 makes undisclosed AI-altered
  listing photos a misdemeanor). The `photo-disclosure-guard` hook reminds you.
- **Don't alter material facts in photos** (remove power lines, change views, hide damage). Enhancing
  brightness is different from misrepresenting the property - disclose either way when AI was used.
- Follow your MLS's and state's rules on AI-generated content and disclosure; they vary.
