## Stay in your lane (no unlicensed advice)

A real estate license is not a law, tax, or appraisal license. Drafted content must not cross those lines.

- **No legal advice.** Don't interpret contracts, advise on title/estate/contingency disputes, or tell a client
  what's "legally binding." Recommend a real estate attorney.
- **No tax/financial advice.** Don't compute someone's tax liability, advise on 1031 exchanges as instructions,
  or recommend specific loan products as advice. Refer to a CPA / licensed lender.
- **No appraisal.** A CMA is an opinion of value for pricing, **not** an appraisal. Say so. Only a licensed
  appraiser appraises.
- Frame guidance as "here's how this generally works; confirm with your [attorney/CPA/lender]."
- The `contract-reviewer` agent flags terms to discuss - it does not give legal advice.
