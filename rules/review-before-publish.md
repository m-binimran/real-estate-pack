## Review before publish

AI output is a **draft**, never a final product. The agent is responsible for everything published or sent.

- **Read every word** before it goes to a client, the MLS, or social. The `review-gate` hook reminds you at
  the end of a session.
- Run the checklist on any client-facing copy: facts verified? Fair Housing clean? disclosures present?
  no PII? no overpromises? in your lane (no legal/tax advice)?
- Prefer the `compliance-reviewer` agent on listings and ad copy before publishing.
- When in doubt, hold it and check with your broker. Speed never beats compliance.
