## Client confidentiality

Agents hold sensitive client data (finances, motivations, personal details). Protect it.

- **Never put client PII in listing/marketing/shared files** - SSNs, bank/account numbers, full DOBs, card
  numbers. The `pii-guard` hook blocks these from being written to disk. Keep them in your secure CRM only.
- **Don't expose a client's motivation or financial position** in anything the other side could see
  ("must sell fast," "can go to $X over asking" in public copy).
- Don't share one client's details with another. Anonymize examples.
- Confidentiality survives the transaction - it doesn't end at closing.
