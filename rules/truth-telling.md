## Truth-telling (no fabricated property facts)

AI hallucinates property details. Misrepresentation is a liability and an ethics violation - the agent owns it.

- **Never invent facts.** Square footage, lot size, year built, school names/ratings, HOA fees, taxes, zoning,
  flood status, permits, comps - if you don't have a verified source, say "verify with the MLS / county / seller,"
  don't guess. The `claim-check-guard` hook flags these.
- **No invented comparables.** A CMA uses real, recent, comparable sales - not made-up addresses or prices.
- **Mark unverified clearly.** Use "[verify]" placeholders rather than confident wrong numbers.
- **Cite the source** for any stat in a market report.
- Don't convert "I drafted copy that says X" into "the home has X." A draft is unverified until the agent checks.
