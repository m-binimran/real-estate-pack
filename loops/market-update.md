# /market-update $ARGUMENTS

Create a market update for the area in `$ARGUMENTS` using the `market-report` skill.

1. **Define the market** (geography, property type, time window).
2. **Pull metrics from a real source** (MLS/public records/named provider): inventory, new + closed sales,
   median price, $/sqft, days on market, months of supply, sale-to-list. Cite source + as-of date.
3. **Read the trend** (buyer's vs. seller's market, price direction) - measured, not hyped.
4. **Make it useful** for buyers and sellers; one headline takeaway.

Output: a short narrative + sourced stats table. Every number cited; anything unverified marked `[verify]`.
No predictions stated as certainty; neighborhood descriptions stay factual (no steering proxies).
