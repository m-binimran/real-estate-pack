# farming-prospecting

Consistency wins a farm. Pick an area, show up with value, track results - compliantly.

## Process
1. **Choose the farm** by objective criteria: turnover rate, absorption, price point, your connection,
   competition - **never** by the demographic makeup of residents (Fair Housing).
2. **Define cadence:** a repeating touch plan (e.g. monthly market update + quarterly value piece + event),
   across mail, door-knock, social, and email to your sphere.
3. **Content mix:** market reports (the `market-report`), just-sold proof, neighborhood value, helpful tips -
   value first, ask second.
4. **Track:** contacts, responses, listings/leads sourced, cost per lead; adjust quarterly.

## Output
- A farm plan: target + why (objective criteria), 6-12 month touch calendar, content per touch, and a simple
  tracking sheet.

## Guardrails
- **Farm selection and messaging must be Fair-Housing-clean** - no targeting or excluding by protected class.
- Mail/email/text follow CAN-SPAM/TCPA (ID, opt-out, consent). Respect do-not-contact.
- Claims in farm content follow truth-telling + no-superlatives.
