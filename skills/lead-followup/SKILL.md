# lead-followup

Stay top-of-mind with helpful, compliant touches - not spam, not steering.

## Process
1. **Segment by stated interest/stage** (new buyer, active buyer, seller-curious, past client) - never by a
   protected class or inferred demographic.
2. **Lead with value:** a relevant listing, a market stat, a useful tip - not "are you ready to buy yet?"
3. **Cadence:** front-load early (day 0, 2, 5), then taper (weekly -> monthly). Mix channels lightly.
4. **Compliance:** every email needs an unsubscribe and your brokerage identification (CAN-SPAM); texts need
   prior consent and STOP handling (TCPA). Honor opt-outs immediately.
5. **Personalize from their stated criteria**, not assumptions about who they are.

## Output
- The sequence (channel, timing, subject/preview, body) with merge fields, plus the opt-out/identification lines.

## Guardrails
- **No Fair Housing steering or profiling** in copy or segmentation.
- Respect CAN-SPAM (unsubscribe + ID) and TCPA (consent + STOP). Don't text without consent.
- Keep lead PII out of shared/marketing files (confidentiality rule).
