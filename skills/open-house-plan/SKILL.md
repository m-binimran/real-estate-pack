# open-house-plan

A repeatable system: promote it, run it safely, capture leads, follow up fast.

## Plan
1. **Promote (3-7 days out):** MLS open-house field, social posts, e-blast to sphere/neighbors, signage plan,
   syndication. Fair-Housing-clean copy.
2. **Prep:** clean/declutter, lights on, temperature, secure valuables and any client PII/documents, set out
   flyers with accurate facts + disclosures.
3. **Sign-in / lead capture:** digital sign-in; capture name, contact, timeline, agent status, financing status -
   with clear consent for follow-up. Don't record protected-class info.
4. **Day-of flow:** greeting, guided highlights (features, not buyer-type), answer with verified facts, safety
   (don't tour alone with strangers; know exits; share location with someone).
5. **Follow-up:** contact every visitor within 24h via the `lead-followup` sequence.

## Output
- A one-page plan: promo checklist + schedule, prep checklist, sign-in fields + consent line, day-of flow,
  safety notes, and the follow-up trigger.

## Guardrails
- Secure all client documents/PII before opening the home (confidentiality + `pii-guard`).
- Marketing and conversation stay Fair-Housing-compliant; follow-up needs consent.
