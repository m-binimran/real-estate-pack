# social-content

Engaging, on-brand, and compliant. The same Fair Housing and accuracy rules apply on social as on the MLS.

## Process
1. **Pick the post type:** just-listed, open house, just-sold (with permission), market stat, tip, testimonial
   (with consent), neighborhood feature, behind-the-scenes.
2. **Hook + value + CTA:** strong first line, useful or appealing middle, clear call to action.
3. **Property posts** describe features, not buyer types. Verified facts only; `[verify]` anything unconfirmed.
4. **Platform fit:** short and visual for Instagram/TikTok, a little longer for Facebook/LinkedIn. Suggest
   hashtags and a caption; note image/video idea.
5. **Disclose** virtual staging / AI-edited media.

## Output
- Caption(s) + hook + CTA + hashtag set, per platform, with any `[verify]` flags and the disclosure line.

## Guardrails
- **Fair Housing applies to social too** - no familial/lifestyle/demographic targeting in copy or ad audience.
- No fabricated facts, no guarantees/superlatives. Get consent for testimonials and sold-price posts.
- Follow ad-platform housing rules (special ad category - limited targeting).
