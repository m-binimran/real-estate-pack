# seller-consultation

Win the listing with a clear, honest plan: price, prep, market, net.

## Cover
1. **Pricing strategy** from the `cma` - a supported range, the market trend, and the risk of overpricing.
   It's an opinion of value, not an appraisal.
2. **Prep & staging:** repairs, declutter, curb appeal, professional photos. Disclose any virtual staging.
3. **Marketing plan:** MLS, syndication, photos/video, social, open house, agent network - concrete and specific.
4. **Seller net sheet (outline):** estimated proceeds = price minus payoff, commissions, closing costs, taxes.
   Use the seller's real figures; flag that exact tax/payoff numbers come from their lender/title/CPA.
5. **Timeline & expectations:** list date, showing process, feedback cadence, offer review.

## Output
- A listing-presentation outline: pricing range + rationale, prep checklist, marketing plan, net-sheet skeleton
  with `[verify]` on figures the agent must confirm.

## Guardrails
- No fabricated numbers; net sheet is an estimate - title/lender/CPA confirm the exacts (not your license).
- Keep the seller's payoff/financials confidential and out of public copy.
- Price honestly; don't buy the listing with an inflated number.
